the Info Capital-page 43

I recently got my hands on a copy of The Information Capital by James Chesire and Oliver Uberti (which is great and you should definitely buy it) and given that I love the city and also love doing plots and stuff with open-source data I thought I’d try my hand at recreating them. This is the first one of these, from page 43 of the book showing commuter flows from the 2011 census. (side note: this is one of the fields of the census that is going to be fucked by Covid-19 in the 2021 update).
